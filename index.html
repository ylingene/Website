Creative Engineer

Coder | Photographer | Creator

Lingene Yang

I am currently a rising senior studying Computer Science Engineering (CSE) at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. Outside of class, I love to code, teach others, and explore my creative side through portrait and landscape photography. I will be a Software Engineer Intern (Pintern) at Pinterest in the summer of 2017 (the first ever from UofM).

I create with C++, Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery; I capture the world with my camera, Photoshop, Lightroom, and Adobe Premiere Pro.

I enjoy crafting dream-like photos of people and places, drawing inspiration from fine art and fashion photography. Wherever I go I seek out interesting locations and create compelling stories around a subject within the environment. My goal is to draw viewers into a dream.
