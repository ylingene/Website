Creative Engineer

Coder | Photographer | Creator

Lingene Yang

I am currently a senior studying Computer Science Engineering (CSE) at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. Outside of class, I love to code, teach others, and explore my creative side through portrait and landscape photography. This past summer I was a Software Engineer Intern (Pintern) at Pinterest, and I'm excited to join Affirm full-time summer 2018.

I create with C++, Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and JQuery; I capture the world with my camera, Photoshop, and Lightroom.

I enjoy crafting dream-like photos of people and places, drawing inspiration from fine art, digital art, and fashion photography. Wherever I go I seek out extraordinary locations and create compelling stories around a subject within the environment. My goal is to draw viewers into a dream.
